Boost the quality of the customer service.

Customer-centric service is becoming more important as the energy and utilities market becomes more efficient. Our PCI-compliant infrastructure, paired with refined customer experience capabilities—including call centre outsource services—assist controlled utilities and deregulated energy providers in increasing operating performance, adhering to regulatory standards, and providing outstanding customer support.





Industry experience and insights.

  • Since 1972, we've been representing energy and utilities customers.
  • Shelbey has over 30 managed and deregulated customers, including half of the top 20 companies in the United States.
  • For both regulated utilities and deregulated electricity providers, we have end-to-end coverage and assistance for residential and industrial customers.




Result you can measure

  • A global electricity corporation saved $1.8 million per year due to a self-service solution.
  • For a major public utility corporation, we reduced total handling time by 30-seconds.
  • Using our call centre consulting capabilities, we increased up-sell conversion rates by 70% and increased revenue by 250 percent for an energy customer.
