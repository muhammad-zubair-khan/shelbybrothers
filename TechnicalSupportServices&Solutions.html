Technical Support Services and Solutions

Providing a high-touch, "no dead-end" service solution for any technological issue.
LEARN MORE


Trouble-free tech support.

Dealing with technical assistance can be a frustrating experience for the customers at times. Throughout the hardware ownership lifecycle, our robust technological service outsourcing programmes seek to have meaningful consumer interactions. Our product experts have the experience, resources, and skills to fix technical challenges in an insanely cool (and painless) manner, whether it's pre-sales, post-sales, or in- or out-of-warranty service.





Experience and insights.

  • Professional support outsourcing activities account for 20% of our total revenue.
  • One of our on-site product laboratories houses over 1,000 of the most cutting-edge products.
  • Shelbey is compatible with almost all smartphones and electrical devices in the "linked home."
  • Every custom knowledgebase contains over 10,000 product SKUs for fast troubleshooting.




Result you can measure

  • Returns on high-end devices have been reduced by 20%. (some product lines as high as 40 percent )
  • With high-touch technological assistance, first-call settlement rates average 85 percent.
  • Improved self-service troubleshooting channels result in a 15% drop in phone support.
